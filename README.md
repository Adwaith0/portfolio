1. **Add Your Profile Photo**:

   - Place your profile photo in the project folder as `profile.jpg`
   - Recommended size: 500x500px or larger (square format)

2. **Update Contact Information**:

   - Open `index.html`
   - Update email, phone number, and social media links
   - Replace `adwaith@example.com` with your actual email
   - Replace `+1 (234) 567-890` with your phone number
   - Add your GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter, and Dribbble URLs

   - Update GitHub and demo links in the Projects section
   - Replace `#` with actual URLs

4. **Add Resume**:

   - Place your resume PDF in the project folder
   - Update the download link in `script.js` (line with download-resume comment)

5. **Open in Browser**:
   - Simply double-click `index.html` to open in your browser
   - Or use a local server for best experience
