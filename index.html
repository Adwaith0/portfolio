Featured UX Project

Connected Plant Care System

Reimagining plant care through smart IoT and intuitive design

Project Overview

A smart plant care application that connects users with their plants through IoT sensors, providing real-time monitoring and personalized care recommendations. The system helps users maintain healthy plants while learning about plant care in an engaging way.

The Challenge

Many plant owners struggle to maintain their plants due to lack of knowledge about specific care requirements and irregular watering schedules. 78% of users forget to water plants regularly, while 82% want automated care reminders.

Key Features

Smart Reminders

Personalized watering and care schedules

Real-time Monitoring

Track soil moisture, light, and temperature

Plant Library

Comprehensive care guides for 500+ plants

Growth Tracking

Visual progress and health analytics
